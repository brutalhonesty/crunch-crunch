Crunch-Crunch
===========


Scrape Crunchyroll Guest Passes from Reddit.com

Email them from your Gmail account to w/e email you want.

Requirements
------------------

* [NodeJS](http://nodejs.org/)
* [Redis](http://redis.io/)
